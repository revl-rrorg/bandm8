Under the Hood

MIDI-native AI that
understands music.

BandM8's generative engine is trained exclusively on MIDI — not audio samples, not loops. It understands musical structure the way a trained musician does: harmony, melody, rhythm, dynamics. The result feels like playing with real people.

The Founder

A musician who became
a label president.
Then built this.

Bob Pfeifer started as a musician — founding guitarist and vocalist of Cleveland new wave band Human Switchboard. He knows what it's like to play with a band, because he was in one.

He went on to become President of Hollywood Records (Disney), one of the most powerful positions in the music industry, working A&R on major artist projects including Alice Cooper's '90s albums. BandM8 is what happens when that experience meets generative AI — built by someone who has lived on both sides of the creative process.

"I've spent my career watching brilliant solo musicians struggle because they couldn't afford a full band. BandM8 changes that. The AI doesn't replace the musician — it completes them." — Bob Pfeifer, Founder & CEO
