The Process

From solo track to full band
in three steps

No music theory required. No studio time. Just upload, choose your vibe, and let BandM8 fill the room.

01 🎤

Upload your track

Drop in your vocals, guitar, piano — whatever you've recorded. A voice memo works. BandM8 analyzes your key, tempo, chord structure, and style automatically.

02 🎛️

Choose your band

Pick the instruments, genre flavor, and energy level. Want a punchy jazz trio? A lush indie-pop wall of sound? A tight funk rhythm section? You direct the session.

03 🎧

Download the stems

Get your full band as individual stems — mix-ready, high quality, yours to use commercially. Take them into any DAW or release as-is.
